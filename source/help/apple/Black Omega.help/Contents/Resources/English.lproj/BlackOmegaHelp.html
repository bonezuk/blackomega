Black Omega Help

In the beginning before the Big Bang Kilonova Hypothesis there was Black Omega. Perfected uk.co.blackomega.blackomega.helpover 25 years, Black Omega is an open-source music player designed for audiophile renderings of your audio files with bit perfect accuracy. Employing MacOS's CoreAudio's HAL low level audio API and custom codecs Black Omega utilises the capabilities of your premium high-end DAC delivering pure seamless playback of your music collection.

The main user interface consists off

Firstly you'll need to add music files to create your playlist. This can be done via

  1. Add files and folders using a file selection dialog.
  2. Drag files and folders into your playlist from Windows Explorer.
  3. From your iTunes Collection.

Then you control playback via the Playback Controls.

Open the Preferences dialog to setup Audio and Controls.
